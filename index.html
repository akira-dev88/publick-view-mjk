About the Party

About Manithaneya Jananayaga Katchi (MJK)

Manithaneya Jananayaga Katchi (MJK) is a political party based in the state of Tamil Nadu, India. The party was formed with the objective of strengthening democratic values, social justice, and people-centric governance. MJK follows a principled political approach that emphasizes humanity, equality, and inclusive development.

The party emerged in February 2016, marking the beginning of a new political movement that focuses on addressing real issues faced by common people, especially at the grassroots level. Headquartered in Chennai, MJK has been actively involved in Tamil Nadu's political landscape since its formation.

Party History

February 2016 - Foundation

Manithaneya Jananayaga Katchi was established with the aim of offering a principled alternative in Tamil Nadu politics, focusing on people-centered governance.

2016 - Legislative Representation

In the 2016 Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Elections, the party gained legislative representation, marking an important milestone in its political journey.

2017-2021 - Organizational Growth

Party expanded its presence across Tamil Nadu, establishing district committees and engaging in public advocacy and community development.

2022-Present - Strengthening Democratic Participation

Continued engagement in public life through political participation, advocacy, and organizational development across the state.

Key Policy Areas

Manithaneya Jananayaga Katchi focuses on practical and people-oriented policies that address everyday challenges faced by citizens of Tamil Nadu.

  • Education & Skill Development: Equal access to quality education, skill training for youth, and support for higher education and employability.
  • Employment & Economic Opportunity: Encouraging job creation, supporting small businesses, farmers, and self-employed individuals, and promoting fair economic growth.
  • Social Welfare & Justice: Protection of social justice, welfare schemes for the underprivileged, and firm opposition to discrimination in any form.
  • Healthcare & Public Services: Strengthening public healthcare systems and ensuring access to basic services for all sections of society.
  • Transparent Governance: Clean administration, accountability in public institutions, and responsible use of public resources.

Additional Policy Areas

Agriculture

Fair pricing and timely support for farmers, improved irrigation and water management, access to modern farming techniques and technology.

Environment

Protection of natural resources and biodiversity, promotion of clean energy and waste management, water conservation and pollution control.

Law, Justice & Security

Equal justice under the law, strengthening the justice delivery system, protection of civil rights and freedoms, community-based approaches to public safety.

Governance & Anti-Corruption

Transparency in administration and public spending, strong anti-corruption mechanisms, digital governance and accountability, ethical conduct in public offices.
